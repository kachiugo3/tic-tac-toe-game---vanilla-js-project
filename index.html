Here Are The Rules

  1. 1. Player 1 is X. Player 2 is O.
  2. 2. Player 1 goes first.
  3. 3. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  4. 4. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  5. 5. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over.
  6. 6. If no player has 3 marks in a row at the end of the game, the game ends in a tie.
  7. 6. Use the reset button to reset scoreline to 0 and start all over again. Use the replay button to play another round.
